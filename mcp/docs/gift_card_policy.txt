RetailCo - Gift Card & Store Credit Policy

1. Gift Cards
- Gift cards are delivered electronically and are redeemable online and in participating stores. They are non-refundable and cannot be redeemed for cash except where required by law.

2. Expiration & Fees
- Gift cards do not expire within typical jurisdictions where non-expiration is required; fees are not charged for standard issuance.

3. Lost or Stolen Cards
- Treat gift cards like cash. Lost or stolen gift cards may be replaced at RetailCo's discretion with proof of purchase and recipient verification.

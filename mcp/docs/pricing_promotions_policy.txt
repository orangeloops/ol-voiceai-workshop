RetailCo - Pricing & Promotions Policy

1. Accuracy & Errors
- RetailCo attempts to provide accurate pricing information but reserves the right to correct errors and cancel orders where a pricing error is identified.

2. Price Adjustments & Price Guarantees
- Price adjustments after purchase are handled per promotional rules; general price-matching guarantees are not offered unless explicitly stated in a promotional campaign.

3. Coupon & Promotional Terms
- Coupons may have blackout dates, category exclusions, and minimum spend requirements; abuse of promotional codes may result in code invalidation or account restrictions.

RetailCo - Loyalty Program Terms

1. Overview
- Members earn points for purchases and engagement. Points can be redeemed for discounts or exclusive benefits subject to program terms.

2. Enrollment & Eligibility
- Enrollment is open to individuals aged 13 and over (or local age threshold). Points are awarded on qualifying purchases after deduction of discounts, returns, and taxes.

3. Expiration & Forfeiture
- Points may expire after extended periods of inactivity; details are published in the membership portal.

4. Program Changes
- RetailCo reserves the right to modify or terminate the program with notice to members.

RetailCo - Supplier Code of Conduct

1. Purpose
RetailCo expects suppliers and manufacturing partners to adhere to ethical labor practices, environmental compliance, and legal standards.

2. Labor & Human Rights
- No forced labor or child labor. Respect for freedom of association and safe working conditions is required.

3. Environmental Compliance
- Suppliers must comply with applicable environmental laws and strive to reduce waste, emissions, and resource consumption.

4. Monitoring & Audits
- RetailCo reserves the right to audit suppliers and require corrective action plans for non-compliance.

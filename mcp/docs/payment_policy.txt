RetailCo - Payment & Pricing Policy

1. Accepted Methods
- Major credit and debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and select regional payment methods where available.
- Gift cards and store credit may be used per the gift card terms.

2. Pricing Errors
- While we strive for accuracy, pricing errors may occur. If an item is listed at an incorrect price, RetailCo reserves the right to refuse or cancel the order and notify the customer.

3. Promotions & Discounts
- Promotional codes are subject to terms and limited to one use per order unless stated otherwise.
- Discounts may not apply to certain categories or Final Sale items.

4. Chargebacks & Fraud
- We investigate chargebacks and fraudulent activity. If a chargeback is found to be invalid, charges may be re-applied and customer accounts may be suspended.
